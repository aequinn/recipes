Simple Vanilla
- 2 cups Heavy Cream
- 2 cups Sugar
- 1 cup whole milk
- 2 tablespoons vanilla

Chill teh cream and milk. Mix all ingredients together with a whisk. And chill
for another hour to as long as another 12 hours.

Pour into Cuisnart icecream maker and let mix for 15 minutes. If still not the
consistency you like, let it run a little longer.

This will make about 6 cups of icecream.
